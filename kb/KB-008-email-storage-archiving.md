# KB-008: Email Storage & Archiving

**Mailbox limit:** **50 GB** per user. Warning banners begin at 45 GB; send is blocked at 50 GB.

**Correct fix:** enable the **online archive** (a second, server-side mailbox with its own 50 GB+) and set the archive policy to move items older than 2 years. Nothing is deleted — items move and remain fully searchable.

**Never advise deleting email as the fix**, especially for legal, accounting, or healthcare clients with retention obligations. If a user's archive is already enabled and full, escalate tier 2 for retention-policy review.
